Woman Of Power

On International Women's Day we celebrate, The strength and power of women, so great, For generations, they've fought and led, Breaking barriers and pushing ahead.

From suffragettes who fought for our vote, To scientists who help us to cope, With challenges both big and small, Women have always given their all.

Mothers, sisters, daughters, and friends, Their love and support never ends, They're there to pick us up when we fall, And help us rise, standing tall.

So on this day, let's honor and cherish, All the women who have fought and perished, And those who continue to make strides, As we strive for equality and all good resides.

Emmanuel Zefack
